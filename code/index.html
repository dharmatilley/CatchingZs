Welcome to Catching Z's

Catching Z's is here to improve your sleep to help you feel better rested. Our site includes a Sleep Tracker, a To-Do List, Playlists, and a Notification Center.

Sleep Tracker

Our Sleep Tracker function will help you track when you are sleeping. By tracking your sleep, you will be able to develop healthier sleep habits such as going to sleep at the same time every night and turning off electronics before bed.

To-Do List

Our To-Do List function will help relieve your stress by allowing you to keep your events and due dates in order. By keeping yourself organized you will relieve your stress and sleep peacefully.

Playlists

For those of you who like to listen to the radio or TV as you sleep look no further! Our Playlists function has all your favorite tunes and sounds. Just simply log into your Spotify or YouTube account and listen to the crashing waves of the ocean, crickets chirping, or even heavy metal. Whatever helps you sleep the best!

Notifications

Does your roomate keep waking you up by coming back late? Let them know you're trying to sleep before they get home so they can be quiet. With our Notification function, you can send out a message alerting them before they disrupt your sleep cycle. If necessary, you can even set a notification to be sent out everyday at your bedtime!
